# M&A Acquisition Pitch Deck Template

## Slide Structure (12-15 slides)

### Slide 1: Cover
**Content:**
- Deal Name: "[Target Company] Acquisition Proposal"
- Presented to: Board of Directors
- Date: [Current Date]
- Confidential watermark

**Design:**
- Navy blue (#1E3A8A) background
- White text, clean typography
- Company logo bottom right

---

### Slide 2: Executive Summary
**Content:**
- Investment Thesis (3 key bullets)
- Deal Value: $XX.XM
- Strategic Rationale (2-3 bullets)
- Key Synergies: $XX.XM
- Timeline: X months to close

**Design:**
- Bullet points, not paragraphs
- Highlight key numbers in color
- Icons for each section

---

### Slide 3: Market Opportunity
**Content:**
- Total Addressable Market (TAM): $XXB
- Target's Market Position: XX% share
- Market Growth Rate: XX% CAGR
- Key Trends (3-4 bullets)
- Competitive Landscape diagram

**Design:**
- Market size chart (bubble or bar)
- Competitive positioning map
- Growth trajectory visualization

---

### Slide 4: Target Company Overview
**Content:**
- Founded: [Year]
- Headquarters: [Location]
- Employees: XX
- Revenue: $XX.XM ARR
- Growth Rate: XX% YoY
- Customers: XXX
- Key Products/Services

**Design:**
- Company logo prominent
- Key metrics in cards
- Timeline or milestones

---

### Slide 5: Financial Performance
**Content:**
- 3-Year Revenue Trend (chart)
- Profitability Metrics
  - Gross Margin: XX%
  - EBITDA Margin: XX%
  - Net Margin: XX%
- Key Performance Indicators
  - Customer Acquisition Cost: $XXX
  - Lifetime Value: $X,XXX
  - LTV/CAC Ratio: X.Xx
- Unit Economics

**Design:**
- Line chart for revenue trend
- Table for margins
- KPI cards with icons

---

### Slide 6: Strategic Rationale
**Content:**
- Why This Target?
  1. [Primary strategic reason]
  2. [Secondary strategic reason]
  3. [Tertiary strategic reason]
- Strategic Fit Analysis
  - Market expansion
  - Technology enhancement
  - Customer access
- Competitive Advantages
- Timing Rationale

**Design:**
- Numbered list for reasons
- Icons for fit areas
- Timeline if relevant

---

### Slide 7: Synergy Analysis
**Content:**

**Revenue Synergies: $XX.XM**
- Cross-sell to existing customers: $X.XM
- Market expansion: $X.XM
- Product bundling: $X.XM
- Pricing optimization: $X.XM

**Cost Synergies: $XX.XM**
- Operational efficiencies: $X.XM
- Technology consolidation: $X.XM
- Overhead reduction: $X.XM
- Vendor negotiations: $X.XM

**Total Synergies: $XX.XM**
**Realization Timeline: 18-24 months**

**Design:**
- Stacked bar chart for synergies
- Waterfall chart showing buildup
- Timeline for realization

---

### Slide 8: Valuation Analysis
**Content:**

**Valuation Approaches:**
- DCF Analysis: $XXM
- Comparable Companies: $XXM
- Precedent Transactions: $XXM

**Proposed Offer: $XXM**
- Valuation Multiple: X.Xx EV/Revenue
- Premium: XX% over last round
- Implied Exit Multiple: X.Xx

**Deal Terms:**
- Cash consideration: XX%
- Stock consideration: XX%
- Earnout potential: $X.XM

**Design:**
- Waterfall chart for valuation
- Comparison table
- Deal structure pie chart

---

### Slide 9: Integration Plan
**Content:**

**Phase 1: Day 1-90 (Stabilization)**
- Communication plan
- Retain key employees
- Ensure business continuity
- Quick wins

**Phase 2: Month 4-6 (Integration)**
- Systems integration
- Process alignment
- Team integration
- Customer migration

**Phase 3: Month 7-12 (Optimization)**
- Synergy capture
- Product roadmap alignment
- Full integration complete

**Phase 4: Year 2+ (Synergy Realization)**
- Full synergy realization
- New product launches
- Market expansion

**Design:**
- Gantt chart or timeline
- Milestone markers
- Color-coded phases

---

### Slide 10: Risk Assessment
**Content:**

**Market Risks** (Medium)
- Competition increasing
- Regulatory changes
- Economic downturn

**Integration Risks** (Low)
- Cultural alignment
- System integration
- Customer retention

**Financial Risks** (Low)
- Revenue shortfall
- Cost overruns
- Synergy delays

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- [Risk 1] → [Mitigation approach]
- [Risk 2] → [Mitigation approach]
- [Risk 3] → [Mitigation approach]

**Design:**
- Risk matrix (likelihood x impact)
- Traffic light colors (red/yellow/green)
- Mitigation strategy table

---

### Slide 11: Management & Team
**Content:**
- Current Leadership Team
  - CEO: [Name, background]
  - CTO: [Name, background]
  - Key executives
- Retention Plan
  - 2-year retention agreements
  - Equity incentives
  - Role clarity post-acquisition

- Photo headshots if available

### Slide 13: Deal Structure
**Content:**

**Purchase Price: $XXM**
- Cash at close: $XXM
- Stock consideration: $XXM

  - Year 1 revenue > $XXM: $XM
  - Year 2 retention > XX%: $XM

**Payment Terms:**
- 80% at close
- 20% earnout (24-month period)

**Closing Conditions:**
- Satisfactory due diligence
- Board approval
- Regulatory clearance

**Design:**
- Pie chart for consideration mix
- Timeline for payments
- Checklist for conditions

---

## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Navy Blue (#1E3A8A)
- **Secondary:** Forest Green (#10B981)  
- **Accent:** Gold (#F59E0B)
- **Text:** Charcoal (#374151)
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Montserrat Bold, 32-36pt
- **Subheadings:** Montserrat SemiBold, 24-28pt
- **Body:** Open Sans Regular, 18-20pt
- **Data:** Roboto Mono, 16-18pt

### Charts
- **Revenue Trends:** Line charts, blue gradient
- **Synergies:** Stacked bar charts, green/blue
- **Timeline:** Gantt chart, color-coded phases
- **Valuation:** Waterfall charts, professional colors
- **Risk Matrix:** 2x2 matrix, traffic light colors

### Layout
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (widescreen)
- **Margins:** 1" all sides
- **Slide Numbers:** Bottom right
- **Logo:** Bottom right, small
- **Confidential:** Header on all slides
