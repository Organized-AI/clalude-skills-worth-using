# M&A Due Diligence Checklist

## Phase 1: Initial Assessment (Days 1-7)

### Financial Due Diligence
- [ ] **3-year financial statements** (audited if available)
- [ ] **Revenue recognition policies** and compliance
- [ ] **Customer concentration analysis** (top 10 customers)
- [ ] **Working capital requirements** and trends
- [ ] **Debt schedule and covenants** review
- [ ] **Tax compliance verification** (federal, state, local)
- [ ] **Contingent liabilities review** (warranties, litigation)

### Operational Due Diligence
- [ ] **Key management assessment** (interviews, backgrounds)
- [ ] **Employee retention analysis** (turnover, satisfaction)
- [ ] **Technology infrastructure audit** (stack, scalability)
- [ ] **Customer satisfaction metrics** (NPS, CSAT, reviews)
- [ ] **Supplier dependency review** (concentration, terms)
- [ ] **Integration complexity assessment** (systems, processes)

### Legal Due Diligence
- [ ] **Corporate structure** and governance documents
- [ ] **Material contracts review** (customers, vendors, partners)
- [ ] **IP ownership verification** (patents, trademarks, copyrights)
- [ ] **Employment agreements** and offer letters
- [ ] **Litigation history** (active, threatened, settled)
- [ ] **Regulatory compliance** (industry-specific)
- [ ] **Data privacy compliance** (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

## Phase 2: Deep Dive (Days 8-21)

### Commercial Due Diligence
- [ ] **Market size validation** (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- [ ] **Competitive landscape analysis** (direct, indirect competitors)
- [ ] **Customer interviews** (top 10, reference checks)
- [ ] **Product roadmap review** (features, timeline, resources)
- [ ] **Go-to-market strategy** (channels, CAC, LTV)
- [ ] **Partnership agreements** (strategic, channel, tech)

### Technical Due Diligence
- [ ] **Code quality assessment** (architecture, debt, security)
- [ ] **Infrastructure scalability** (AWS, servers, databases)
- [ ] **Security audit** (vulnerabilities, penetration testing)
- [ ] **Data architecture** (storage, pipelines, governance)
- [ ] **Development process** (CI/CD, testing, deployment)
- [ ] **Technical team assessment** (skills, capacity, gaps)

### HR Due Diligence
- [ ] **Organization chart** and reporting structure
- [ ] **Compensation analysis** (salaries, equity, benefits)
- [ ] **Key person dependencies** (founder, CTO, etc.)
- [ ] **Retention agreements** (necessary for key employees)
- [ ] **Culture assessment** (values, practices, fit)
- [ ] **Benefits and perks** (health, 401k, etc.)

## Phase 3: Final Validation (Days 22-30)

### Synergy Validation
- [ ] **Revenue synergy opportunities** (cross-sell, upsell)
- [ ] **Cost synergy opportunities** (consolidation, efficiency)
- [ ] **Integration timeline** (realistic, phased approach)
- [ ] **Day 1 readiness** (critical systems, processes)
- [ ] **100-day plan** (quick wins, priorities)

### Risk Assessment
- [ ] **Market risks** (competition, disruption, regulation)
- [ ] **Execution risks** (team, technology, operations)
- [ ] **Financial risks** (revenue, costs, working capital)
- [ ] **Integration risks** (cultural, technical, operational)
- [ ] **Mitigation strategies** (for each identified risk)

### Final Approval
- [ ] **Investment committee memo** (recommendation, rationale)
- [ ] **Board presentation** (strategic fit, financials, risks)
- [ ] **LOI finalization** (price, terms, conditions)
- [ ] **Definitive agreement** (SPA drafting, negotiation)

## Red Flags Checklist

### Critical (Deal Breakers)
- ❌ **Fraud or misrepresentation** in financials or operations
- ❌ **Material litigation** with high likelihood of adverse outcome
- ❌ **IP not owned** or significant IP disputes
- ❌ **Customer concentration >50%** with at-risk accounts
- ❌ **Technology fundamentally broken** or unscalable
- ❌ **Regulatory non-compliance** with significant penalties

### High Concern (Require Mitigation)
- ⚠️ **Declining revenue trends** (>10% YoY)
- ⚠️ **High customer churn** (>20% annually)
- ⚠️ **Key person dependency** (founder-led sales/product)
- ⚠️ **Technical debt** (significant refactoring needed)
- ⚠️ **Weak financial controls** (no audit, poor processes)
- ⚠️ **Cultural misalignment** (values, practices)

### Medium Concern (Monitor)
- ⚠️ **Modest revenue concentration** (25-50% top customer)
- ⚠️ **Competitive pressure** (new entrants, pricing)
- ⚠️ **Moderate technical debt** (manageable scope)
- ⚠️ **Some employee turnover** (10-15% annually)
- ⚠️ **Minor compliance gaps** (easily remedied)

## Due Diligence Team

### Internal Team
- **Deal Lead**: Overall coordination
- **CFO/Finance**: Financial DD
- **CTO/Engineering**: Technical DD
- **VP Product**: Product/roadmap DD
- **VP Sales/Marketing**: Commercial DD
- **Legal**: Legal/IP DD
- **HR**: People/culture DD

### External Advisors
- **Investment Bank**: Valuation, market analysis
- **Legal Counsel**: Transaction docs, regulatory
- **Accounting Firm**: Financial audit, tax
- **Technical Consultant**: Code review, security
