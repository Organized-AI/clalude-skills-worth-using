# M&A Startup Validation Frameworks

## Framework 1: Acquisition Target Assessment

### Market Opportunity (25 points)

- [ ] **TAM >$1B** (10 pts)
  - Total Addressable Market exceeds $1B
  - Growing market >15% YoY
  - Clear market positioning

- [ ] **Market Growth >15%** (8 pts)
  - Year-over-year growth rate
  - Industry momentum
  - Adoption trends

- [ ] **Competitive Position** (7 pts)
  - Differentiation clear
  - Defensible moat
  - Market share trajectory

### Financial Health (25 points)

- [ ] **Revenue >$1M ARR** (10 pts)
  - Annual Recurring Revenue
  - Revenue quality
  - Customer concentration

- [ ] **Gross Margin >60%** (8 pts)
  - Unit economics
  - Scalability
  - Cost structure

- [ ] **Path to Profitability** (7 pts)
  - Burn rate
  - Runway
  - Growth efficiency

### Strategic Fit (25 points)

- [ ] **Synergy Potential >$5M** (10 pts)
  - Revenue synergies
  - Cost synergies
  - Market expansion

- [ ] **Customer Overlap >20%** (8 pts)
  - Cross-sell opportunities
  - Market adjacency
  - Channel compatibility

- [ ] **Tech Stack Compatible** (7 pts)
  - Integration complexity
  - Platform alignment
  - Data compatibility

### Team & Execution (25 points)

- [ ] **Experienced Founders** (10 pts)
  - Domain expertise
  - Previous exits
  - Industry relationships

- [ ] **Key Hires Secured** (8 pts)
  - C-suite complete
  - Technical leadership
  - Sales/ops maturity

- [ ] **Strong Retention >90%** (7 pts)
  - Employee turnover
  - Culture fit
  - Compensation structure

## Scoring Guidelines

### Excellent (90-100 points)
- Move quickly, strong target
- LOI within 2 weeks
- Competitive process expected

### Good (75-89 points)
- Investigate specific concerns
- Likely proceed to full DD
- Address gaps in diligence

### Caution (60-74 points)
- Significant due diligence needed
- Higher risk tolerance required
- Consider strategic alternatives

### Pass (<60 points)
- Pass unless compelling strategic reason
- Major concerns outweigh benefits
- Better targets likely available

## Framework 2: SaaS-Specific Rubric

### Key Metrics
- **ARR Growth**: >100% (excellent), 50-100% (good), <50% (concern)
- **Net Revenue Retention**: >120% (excellent), 100-120% (good), <100% (concern)
- **LTV/CAC**: >5x (excellent), 3-5x (good), <3x (concern)
- **Magic Number**: >1.0 (excellent), 0.75-1.0 (good), <0.75 (concern)
- **Rule of 40**: >40% (excellent), 20-40% (good), <20% (concern)

---

**Use these frameworks to conduct systematic validation of acquisition targets.**
