## Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Navy Blue (#1E3A8A) - Trust, stability
- **Secondary**: Forest Green (#10B981) - Growth, success
- **Accent**: Gold (#F59E0B) - Value, premium
- **Background**: White/Light Gray (#F9FAFB)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat Bold, 32-36pt
- **Body**: Open Sans Regular, 18-20pt
- **Data**: Roboto Mono, 16pt

### Chart Styles
- **Revenue**: Line chart, blue gradient
- **Synergies**: Waterfall chart, blue/green
- **Timeline**: Gantt style, color-coded
- **Valuation**: Football field, gray bars

### Layout
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- 1" margins all sides
- Consistent header/footer
- Logo: bottom right
- Page numbers: bottom center
