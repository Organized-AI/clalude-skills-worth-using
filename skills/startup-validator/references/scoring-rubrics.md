## Scoring Guidelines

### How to Use These Rubrics

1. **Select Appropriate Rubric**: Choose based on company's primary business model
2. **Score Each Metric**: Assign points based on thresholds
3. **Weight Categories**: Different metrics may have different importance
4. **Aggregate Score**: Sum weighted scores for overall assessment
5. **Document Rationale**: Note key factors influencing scores

### Scoring System
- **Excellent**: 9-10 points (90-100%)
- **Good**: 7-8 points (70-89%)
- **Fair**: 5-6 points (50-69%)
- **Poor**: 0-4 points (0-49%)

### Red Flags That Override Scores

Regardless of scoring, automatic caution for:
- Legal issues or litigation
- Regulatory compliance failures
- Financial irregularities
- Key team departures
- Major customer losses
- Security breaches
- IP disputes

### Final Recommendation Framework

**Overall Score 90-100**: **STRONG BUY**
- Move quickly, likely competitive
- Standard DD process
- Prepare offer immediately

**Overall Score 75-89**: **BUY**
- Solid opportunity
- Standard to enhanced DD
- Proceed with confidence

**Overall Score 60-74**: **HOLD/INVESTIGATE**
- Significant questions to answer
- Deep DD required
- May still proceed with mitigation

**Overall Score <60**: **PASS**
- High risk
- Fundamental concerns
- Only proceed with extraordinary circumstances
