# M&A Validation Frameworks

## 4-Dimension Scoring Framework

### Market Opportunity (25 points)

#### TAM Analysis (10 points)
- **10 pts**: TAM >$10B, rapidly growing
- **8 pts**: TAM $1-10B, steady growth
- **6 pts**: TAM $500M-$1B, stable
- **4 pts**: TAM $100-500M, limited growth
- **0 pts**: TAM <$100M or declining

#### Market Growth (8 points)
- **8 pts**: >30% YoY growth
- **6 pts**: 15-30% YoY growth
- **4 pts**: 5-15% YoY growth
- **2 pts**: 0-5% YoY growth
- **0 pts**: Negative growth

#### Competitive Position (7 points)
- **7 pts**: Market leader or strong #2
- **5 pts**: Top 5 player, differentiated
- **3 pts**: Niche player, defensible position
- **1 pt**: Weak position, commoditized

### Financial Health (25 points)

#### Revenue Scale (10 points)
- **10 pts**: >$10M ARR
- **8 pts**: $5-10M ARR
- **6 pts**: $1-5M ARR
- **4 pts**: $500K-$1M ARR
- **0 pts**: <$500K ARR

#### Growth Rate (8 points)
- **8 pts**: >200% YoY
- **6 pts**: 100-200% YoY
- **4 pts**: 50-100% YoY
- **2 pts**: 20-50% YoY
- **0 pts**: <20% YoY

#### Unit Economics (7 points)
- **7 pts**: LTV/CAC >5x, Payback <6mo
- **5 pts**: LTV/CAC 3-5x, Payback 6-12mo
- **3 pts**: LTV/CAC 2-3x, Payback 12-18mo
- **1 pt**: LTV/CAC <2x or Payback >18mo

### Strategic Fit (25 points)

#### Synergy Potential (10 points)
- **10 pts**: >$10M identified synergies
- **8 pts**: $5-10M synergies
- **6 pts**: $2-5M synergies
- **4 pts**: $500K-$2M synergies
- **0 pts**: Minimal synergies

#### Customer Overlap (8 points)
- **8 pts**: >40% customer overlap
- **6 pts**: 20-40% overlap
- **4 pts**: 10-20% overlap
- **2 pts**: <10% overlap

#### Technology Compatibility (7 points)
- **7 pts**: Perfect stack alignment
- **5 pts**: Compatible with minor adjustments
- **3 pts**: Moderate integration work
- **1 pt**: Significant rebuild required

### Team & Execution (25 points)

#### Founder Experience (10 points)
- **10 pts**: Serial successful founders
- **8 pts**: Prior relevant experience
- **6 pts**: First-time, strong track record
- **4 pts**: Limited experience
- **0 pts**: Weak or problematic background

#### Team Quality (8 points)
- **8 pts**: Complete exec team, low turnover
- **6 pts**: Strong team, some gaps
- **4 pts**: Adequate team, hiring needs
- **2 pts**: Weak team, high turnover

#### Retention Probability (7 points)
- **7 pts**: >95% key employee retention
- **5 pts**: 85-95% retention
- **3 pts**: 70-85% retention
- **1 pt**: <70% retention or major concerns

## Overall Score Interpretation

### 90-100: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent Acquisition Target
**Action**: Move quickly to secure
- Rare combination of market opportunity and execution
- Multiple strategic benefits
- Low integration risk
- Competitive situation likely

**Recommended approach:**
- Fast-track due diligence
- Consider premium valuation
- Secure exclusivity early
- Aggressive retention packages

### 75-89: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good Target, Some Concerns
**Action**: Proceed with standard process
- Strong overall profile
- Address concerns in due diligence
- Negotiate based on risk factors
- Plan mitigation strategies

**Recommended approach:**
- Standard due diligence timeline
- Market-rate valuation
- Performance-based earnout consideration
- Key person retention focus

### 60-74: ⭐⭐⭐ Proceed with Caution
**Action**: Deep dive required
- Significant concerns to validate
- Higher integration risk
- May require transformation
- Consider alternatives

**Recommended approach:**
- Extended due diligence
- Conservative valuation
- Structured earnout (30-40%)
- Contingency planning

### <60: ⭐⭐ High Risk - Likely Pass
**Action**: Investigate further or pass
- Multiple red flags
- Unclear path to value creation
- High execution risk
- Better opportunities likely exist

**Recommended approach:**
- Limited resources on further validation
- Asset deal vs. equity consideration
- Focus on other targets
- Keep as backup option only

## Industry-Specific Adjustments

### SaaS Companies
**Adjust scoring for:**
- Revenue growth: +20% weight
- Gross margin: Must be >70%
- Net retention: >110% ideal
- Churn: <5% annual max

### E-commerce
**Adjust scoring for:**
- GMV growth over profit
- Repeat purchase rate critical
- CAC payback <12 months
- Inventory management key

### Marketplace
**Adjust scoring for:**
- Network effects strength
- Supply/demand balance
- Take rate sustainability
- Fraud/trust metrics

### Enterprise Software
**Adjust scoring for:**
- Customer concentration risk
- Professional services dependency
- Sales cycle complexity
- Implementation success rate
